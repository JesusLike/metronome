Metronome trainer

How to:

Type BPM value into the box and click on the "Start button". Then you can just listen metronome clicks for excercising or check your accuracy by hitting any key trying to catch the beat. With each hit you will see your mark (from "Fail" to "Excellent"). Green text color means that you've done it much accurately. Red means that you've been too late and blue means "too early". Enjoy!
